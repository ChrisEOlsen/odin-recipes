Recipe For Lasagna

Lasagna is the name of one of the oldest and best-known pasta shapes. It is usually rectangular or ribbon shaped and thicker than tagliatelle!

This dish is cheesy, and if done right, has a lot of sauce and just the right amount of protein.

Recipe for Lasagna:

  • Box of uncooked lasagna
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Oregano

  2. Make the lasagna
  3. Eat the lasagna
